Pomodoro

A calm open-source focus app for macOS

Pomodoro is a lightweight focus tool designed for people who want to work without distraction. It combines a clean timer, gentle focus flow sessions, and task awareness in a quiet interface.

Open-source core, thoughtfully evolving.
