# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 15, 2026**

## 1. Overview

Orchestrana™ ("Orchestrana," "we," "our," or "the Service") is a productivity app for planning work, focusing in timed sessions, reviewing progress, and using optional AI-assisted features. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, when we share it, and the choices available to you.

This policy applies to the Orchestrana app, the website at https://orchestrana.app, and related cloud services that support accounts, subscriptions, AI features, and security.

## 2. Data We Collect

### Account Information

If you create an account or sign in, we may collect information needed to authenticate you and operate your account, such as your email address, display name, profile image, provider identifier, Firebase user ID, and authentication session data. The exact information depends on the sign-in method you choose, such as Sign in with Apple, Google Sign-In, or email sign-in.

### Subscription and Entitlement Information

If you purchase or restore a subscription, we may process subscription status, product identifier, transaction identifier, renewal status, expiration date, entitlement tier, and related verification data. Payments are handled by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

### Local App Data

Orchestrana is designed to be local-first. Tasks, timer settings, session history, planning state, notes, calendar-derived context, onboarding state, and preferences may be stored locally on your device using app storage, Application Support files, UserDefaults, or Keychain where appropriate.

Some local data may be sent to our backend only when you sign in, verify subscriptions, use cloud-backed AI features, request support, or explicitly use a feature that requires server processing.

### Usage and Diagnostic Data

We may collect limited usage, quota, entitlement, request, error, and diagnostic data to operate the Service, prevent abuse, improve reliability, and understand feature health. This may include app version, device or platform information, request timestamps, feature type, authentication status, AI usage totals, and server logs.

### Optional AI Input Data

If you use AI features, we may process the content you submit for that specific request. This may include task titles, task descriptions, notes, scheduling constraints, productivity metrics, calendar availability, focus history summaries, or other context you choose to provide to generate AI planning, scheduling, drafting, summaries, or insights.

You should avoid submitting highly sensitive personal information to AI features unless you are comfortable with that information being processed to provide the requested feature.

### Website Data

When you visit our website, we may process standard technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, referring page, pages visited, language preference, and request logs. If you submit a form or contact us, we collect the information you provide.

## 3. How We Use Data

We use data to:

- Provide and maintain the app, website, and account features
- Authenticate users and maintain secure sessions
- Verify subscriptions, restore purchases, and enforce entitlements
- Provide AI planning, AI drafting, AI scheduling, AI summaries, and productivity insights when requested
- Keep local-first workflows responsive and reliable
- Enforce quotas, rate limits, abuse prevention, and security protections
- Diagnose errors, improve performance, and support users
- Communicate with you about support, account, legal, or service matters
- Comply with applicable law, platform requirements, and App Store review requirements

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your AI input data for advertising. We do not use your data to train our own AI models.

## 4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to operate the Service. These providers process data according to their own terms and privacy policies and only as needed for the relevant feature.

Third-party services may include:

- **Apple** for App Store billing, subscriptions, purchase restoration, Sign in with Apple, StoreKit, and App Store server notifications
- **Firebase / Google Cloud** for authentication, backend infrastructure, Cloud Functions, account state, entitlement checks, quota tracking, and security
- **Google** for Google Sign-In and, where used, Gemini-family AI processing
- **OpenRouter** for AI routing to supported model providers
- **AI model providers** such as DeepSeek or Google Gemini when you use AI features that require those models
- **GitHub** for public repository links and open-source project pages

When AI features are used, relevant request content may be sent through our backend to AI providers or routing services to generate the requested output.

## 5. Data Storage and Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data, including platform security controls, Firebase security practices, authenticated requests, server-side entitlement checks, limited secret access, and secure storage mechanisms such as Keychain for sensitive local session data where appropriate.

No system is perfectly secure. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device, Apple ID, account credentials, and any third-party accounts used to sign in.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain account, subscription, usage, diagnostic, and support data only for as long as needed to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent abuse, enforce agreements, and maintain accurate subscription or entitlement records.

Local app data remains on your device unless you delete it, reset the app, remove the app, or use a feature that sends data to the backend.

## 7. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete certain personal information. You may also have the right to object to or restrict certain processing.

You can:

- Manage or cancel subscriptions through your App Store account settings
- Restore purchases in the app
- Delete or edit local tasks, notes, and app data where the app provides controls
- Stop using optional AI features
- Revoke app permissions such as Calendar, Reminders, or Notifications in system settings
- Contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of account-linked data

Some records may be retained when necessary for security, fraud prevention, billing, tax, legal compliance, or legitimate operational needs.

## 8. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13 or the minimum age required by local law. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us so we can take appropriate action.

## 9. International Processing

Your information may be processed in countries other than where you live. Those countries may have different data protection laws. We take reasonable steps to ensure appropriate protections for data processed by our service providers.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the Service, legal requirements, App Store requirements, or third-party services change. The updated version will be posted on our website with a new "Last updated" date.
